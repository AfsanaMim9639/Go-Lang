Logical Operators:
    !(logical NOT)
    ||(logical or)
    &&(logical)


Bitwise Operators:
    &(bitwise AND)
    |(bitwise OR)
    ^(bitwise XOR)
    &^(BIT CLEAR, AND NOT)
    <<(left shift)
    >>(right shift)


Comparison Operators:

    ==(equal)
    !=(not equal)
    < (less than)
    <=(less than or equal to)
    >(greater than)
    >=(greater than or equal to)
